Who are we?

Here at Jimmy's Lemons, we take pride in our high-quality Lemon products and services. Our mission is to provide the world with affordable lemons and give life to people. Our job is only a success when the whole world has access to lemons.

All of our lemons are locally grown in the United States and shipped via American Companies around the World. A few of our services include Lemon Products for sale, Influencer Partnership Program, Corporation Exclusive Partnership Program. The company ensures that all services are promptly met with excellent customer service.

Our Services

Core Services

Lemon Farms
We take pride in our local farms, where we grow anything from Lemons to Limes! We rent out small plots of our farms to any group of enthusiasts who want to have their own farm and want to take some of their own pride homes.
Food Delivery
We aim to deliver any products ordered anywhere in the World! We sell Lemonade, Lemons, Lemon Juice, Jerry's Sublimed Limes, Jimmy's Special Lemon Sauce, and Lemon Plushies!
Catering
We provide catering coming from our ghost kitchens located across the Nation! These kitchens aren't accessible to the public, but instead, we bring the food to your party or event! Click here to see our menu!

Corporation Services
