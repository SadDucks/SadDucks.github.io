Who are we?

Here at Jimmy's Lemons, we take pride in our high-quality Lemon products and services. Our mission is to provide the world with affordable citrus and bring joy to our consumers. Our job is only a success when everyone in the galaxy has access to citrus.

All of our lemons are locally grown in the United States and shipped via American Companies. Our services include: Lemon Products for sale, Influencer Partnership Program, Corporation Exclusive Partnership Program and more. We strive to provide our services with excellent customer service.


Our Services

Core Services

Lemon Farms
We take pride in our local farms, where we grow anything from Lemons to Limes! We rent out small plots of our farms to any group of enthusiasts who want to have their own farm and want to take some of their own pride homes.
Food Delivery
We aim for our products to be accessible worldwide, and we currently ship to all 195 countries! Our products include: fresh citrus fruit, Organic Lemonade in various flavors, citrus juices, Jerry's Sublime Lime Sauce, Jimmy's Splendid Lemon Sauce, Jimmy's Lemons merch, and more.
Catering
We provide catering coming from our ghost kitchens located across the Nation. These kitchens aren't accessible to the public, but instead, we bring the food to your party or event. Click here to see our menu.

Corporation Services

Retail
We allow retailers to sell our products from all of our farms, bakeries, and ghost kitchens.
Resturants
We partner with restaurants to offer our special Jimmy's Lemon Sauce to enhance their customer's experience!

Indivudal Services

Influencer Program
Currently, our Influencer Program has a limited amount of sponsorships available. Our partners will gain access to a commission and beta products to test in return for advertisement to their audience. Interested parties are free to reach out, and we'll work with you to the best of our ability.
